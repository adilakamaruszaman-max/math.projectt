                        Malaysian Agrifood Sector Analysis
                    
                    

                        Optimizing the Post-Harvest Window of MD2 Pineapples
                    

                    

                        In Malaysia's highly lucrative agro-product export market (driven by the Malaysian Pineapple Industry Board - MPIB), the MD2 variety is prized for its golden pulp and high sweetness. For food processors, the sugar concentration—measured in Total Soluble Solids ($^{\circ}\text{Brix}$)—dictates pasteurization profiles, enzyme stability (bromelain), and shelf-life logistics. 
                    

                    

                        By applying calculus, agro-technologists use the first derivative $f'(x)$ to calculate the exact speed of sugar accumulation. This allows us to predict when the crop reaches peak metabolic activity (inflection point where $f''(x) = 0$) and schedule optimal harvest intervals relative to weather and fertilization variables.
